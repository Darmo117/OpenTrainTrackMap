What is featured on the map?

Railway-related things that are current or that have existed in the past. It includes tracks, stations, bridges, signals, etc.

Anything not directly related to railways, like mountains, cities, forests, roads, seas, countries, etc.

Basic terms for editing

{{ context.site_name }} has some specific terms that you will encounter when editing.

  • A node is a point in the map, they are mostly encountered when drawing polylines and polygons.
  • A polyline is a sequence of line segments, like a train track or a wall.
  • A polygon is a zone delimited by a closed polyline,like a building or an area.
